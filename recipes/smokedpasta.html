Smoked Pasta

Description

It's a warm bellied Italian classic, with layers of zesty sausage, oozing cheese

and a flood of full-bodied smoke making this dish perfect for a traditional Sunday gathering.

Ingredients

  • Ziti, cooked 1 minute less than directions
  • Spaghettin Sauce
  • Garlic minced
  • Italian sausage, cooked

Steps

  1. Set temp to 450f
  2. In a large bowl, pour spaghettin sauce over pasta
  3. coat a 9x3x2-inch baking dish with nonstick cooking spray
  4. Pour half the pasta mixture into prep dish. Sprinkle with cheese
  5. Bake in traeger until cheese is golden brown
