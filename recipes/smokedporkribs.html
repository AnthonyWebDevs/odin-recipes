Smoked Pork Ribs

Description

Don't have time to wait around on ribs? These hot and fast baby backs are the answer.

Traeger Pork & Poultry Rub-seasoned ribs are given an apple juice spritz and cooked over cherry wood for deep flavor and rich color.

Ingredients

  • Baby back ribs
  • Traeger POrk and Poultry rub
  • Apple Juice

Steps

  1. Preheat to 300F
  2. Remove Membrane from the back of each rack of ribs
  3. Place ribs on grill grates
  4. Remove from grill and let rest 10mins
